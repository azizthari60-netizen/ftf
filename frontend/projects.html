LOOK OUT OUR CAUSES & HELP US

When community members lead change, the change lasts and lasts.

WATER PROJECT

Falah-e-Thar Foundation Pakistan (FTF) has taken initiative to support communities through the "Water Project". A comprehensive water infrastructure has been established to facilitate these communities. This program has state of the art facilities like wells, hand pumps, and water filtration systems. Anyone can be a part of this service to transform the community's life for good.

DONATE NOW

FOOD PROJECT

Falah-e-Thar Foundation (FTF) has come up with a new social project to feed the underprivileged people and those unable to get basic food. Our Foundation is providing free meals daily to needy people. We are setting up new "Food Project" initiatives across the country. You can be a part of this by donating a single meal to as much as you can spare for the betterment of the community.

DONATE NOW

EDUCATION PROJECT

Falah-e-Thar Foundation believes that every person deserves dignity, love and equal opportunities. In order for the entire world to truly become equal, it needs to start with education. Underprivileged children often have no access to quality education and to curb this, Falah-e-Thar Foundation is setting up well-equipped schools in Tharparkar providing quality education to every child.

DONATE NOW

HEALTH PROJECT

The Health Project aims to provide accessible healthcare services to remote communities in Tharparkar. Our initiative focuses on preventive care, health education, and medical treatment for underprivileged populations. We are establishing medical camps and health clinics to ensure that every individual has access to basic healthcare services and can live a healthy life.

DONATE NOW

ORPHAN PROJECT

The Orphan Project is dedicated to providing shelter, care, and education to orphaned children. We believe every child deserves love, security, and opportunities to reach their potential. Our program provides food, clothing, education, and emotional support to help these vulnerable children build a better future. By supporting this project, you directly impact the lives of children in need.

DONATE NOW

MASAJID PROJECT

The Masajid Project focuses on constructing and maintaining mosques and religious centers in remote areas. These facilities serve as spiritual hubs for communities, providing space for worship and religious education. We are committed to supporting the spiritual growth of communities while creating gathering places that strengthen social bonds and provide essential services to residents.

DONATE NOW

MODEL CITY PROJECT

The Model City Project represents our vision for sustainable community development in Tharparkar. This comprehensive initiative combines modern infrastructure, education, healthcare, and economic opportunities to create a model community. We are building a sustainable ecosystem that provides residents with access to basic services, employment opportunities, and improved living conditions.

DONATE NOW

WEDDING PROJECT

The Wedding Project provides financial support to underprivileged families to help them conduct their wedding ceremonies with dignity. We understand that weddings are important cultural events, and financial constraints should not prevent families from celebrating this joyous occasion. Our program assists families in need by providing resources to ensure everyone can have a memorable wedding celebration.

DONATE NOW

EMPOWERMENT PROJECT

The Empowerment Project focuses on skill development and economic empowerment of marginalized communities. We provide vocational training, business development support, and microfinance opportunities to help individuals become self-reliant. Through this program, we equip people with the skills and resources needed to generate sustainable income and improve their economic status.
