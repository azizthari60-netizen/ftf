Foundation Launches New Water Well Project

Published: January 15, 2024

Falah-e-Thar Foundation has successfully launched a new water well project in remote villages of Tharparkar. This initiative aims to provide clean drinking water to over 500 families in the region.

Education Program Reaches 400 Students

Published: December 20, 2023

Our Educate Thar Program has reached a milestone of 400 students enrolled in community schools. The program provides free education, books, and learning materials to underprivileged children.

The Feed Thar Program has been expanded to serve 700 meals monthly across 15 villages. This expansion was made possible through generous donations from our supporters.
